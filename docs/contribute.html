Contribute to the Project

We would love for you to contribute to the project! Your contributions will help make this project more valuable and impactful for students who rely on it!

This project is a free and open-source initiative aimed at providing valuable study resources for CBSE Class 11 & 12 students. If you have any study materials, question papers, or chapter-wise notes that could benefit others, we encourage you to contribute. Your work can help students prepare for their exams in an efficient and structured way.

To contribute, please submit your files through the following Google Form. We welcome submissions in various formats like PDFs, DOCs, or PPT Files. Please make sure that you send only Question Papers or Chapterwise Study Materials that are non-copyrighted or publicly available. We are unable to accept any premium or copyrighted content, as this project follows strict guidelines to ensure that the materials we provide are freely accessible to everyone.

Contributing to the project is easy, and every submission counts! Not only will you be helping students, but you'll also be giving back to the community. Whether you're a student, teacher, or educational enthusiast, your contributions are valued and play an essential role in helping us build this platform. Your name and contribution may even be credited on the website to give you the recognition you deserve.

Remember, this project thrives because of your support and contributions. We ask that you respect copyright laws, and refrain from submitting any material that is owned, paid for, or considered explicit or inappropriate.
