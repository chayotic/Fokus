About

This website is a free resource designed to help computer science students studying for the CBSE Class 11 & 12 exams. We provide high-quality study materials, sample papers, and additional resources that can help you in your exam preparation. All materials shared on this website are intended to be freely accessible educational resources. We encourage users to contribute content that is either publicly available or authorized for educational use, and we do not accept copyrighted or premium materials.

This website is open-source and hosted on GitHub. Feel free to explore the project and contribute to its growth.
